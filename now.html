What I'm doing now

Updated April 2019

I'm living in Jacksonville, FL with my dog Holden, working on my company, Write.as / A Bunch Tell.

During the day I'm:

  • thinking about blogging, digital privacy, and what ethical software products / organizations should look like
  • writing about what I come up with
  • putting things into practice with Write.as
  • developing tools and doing other custom work on WriteFreely, the open source software behind Write.as
  • creating a suite of products with Snap.as, Read.as, and others

For fun, I'm:

  • reading The Age of Surveillance Capitalism by Shoshana Zuboff — highly recommended for anyone concerned about the business models of Facebook, Google, et al.
  • reading about urban planning and architecture — currently, Streets for People by Bernard Rudofsky
  • listening to Flume, Bayonne, cln, Petit Biscuit, and other electronica
  • learning French
  • playing Zelda: Breath of the Wild

health

The past several years of professional sedentarianism and the discovery of hoppy beer in my early 20's left me a little overweight. So late last year I started eating a low-carb / keto diet and combined it with intermittent fasting. This month I finally started exercising regularly.

I was fasting for 16 hours and eating for 8 every day. Now I'll switch it to 20 hours of fasting, 4 hours of eating every once in a while. I'm still figuring out what I can manage while still biking to the gym and lifting weights.

I start the day with coffee and some coconut oil, then a smoothie for lunch (coconut milk, avocado, spinach, kale, chia seeds, a bit of protein powder and some berries or banana), and finally fish or eggs for dinner. So far, I've lost 20 pounds (9 kg), but I still have a bit to go.

future

I'm hoping to move soon — Florida isn't for me. I like Jacksonville for what it is, but I'd like to move to a more thriving city that's farther from the equator. Right now the potential list includes DC, New York City, Paris, Berlin, and Stockholm. But I'm open to any locale with four seasons and, preferably, at least one hill.
